Dr Matt Clark - Personal Website

Research Scientist - Physical Oceanography | National Oceanography Centre | Southampton, UK.


I am a physical oceanographer interested in the changing dynamics and hydrography of the North Atlantic Ocean over the past 4+ decades. I'm also interested in the biogeochemical consequences of the observed changes. I completed my PhD in Physical Oceanography in January 2023, with my thesis entitled "Changing Atlantic Influences on Northwest European Shelf Seas".

Outside of academia, I am an experienced hiker. I previously sat on the committee of Southampton University Hillwalking Club for 4 years and have organised and led day trips of up to 100 students around Hampshire and the Isle of Wight. I have also led small groups on more challenging weekend-long trips to some of the more remote areas of the UK. During the weekends and my days off, you can usually find me deep in the countryside with my camera.

About this site

The aim of this site is to provide a hub of all my work and research-related web profiles. I also occasionally use this site to blog about research activities.

I have built this website myself. It is a "static" HTML5 website with Bootstrap, based on the free open source templates and guides by Start Bootstrap. Thanks to Bootstrap's use of Flexbox, the website displays well on almost any screen size. It's cookie, tracking/analytics and advertising free, so fully GDPR compliant. The site is currently freely hosted by GitHub Pages.
